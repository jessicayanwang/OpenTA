## GRADING POLICY
Your final grade is calculated as follows:
- Problem Sets: 50%
- Final Project: 30%
- Quizzes: 20%

All work must be submitted by the specified deadlines to receive credit.

## LATE POLICY
Each student has 3 late days to use throughout the semester. A late day allows you to submit an assignment up to 24 hours late without penalty. You may use multiple late days on a single assignment.

After exhausting your late days, late submissions will receive a 10% penalty per day, up to 3 days. Assignments more than 3 days late will not be accepted.

## COLLABORATION POLICY
You may discuss problem sets with classmates, but you must write your own code. Copying code from others or online sources is prohibited and will result in academic integrity violations.

You may use AI tools like ChatGPT for learning, but not for generating solutions to graded assignments.
